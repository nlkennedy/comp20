Save the Giant Tortoises

Home Services Contact

Giant tortoises are on the vulnerable level of the endangered species list, according to the World Wildlife Fund, which means that they have a high risk of extinction in the wild. They are threatened by new predator species, such as dogs and cats that are introduced to the islands. Tortoises are also in competition for grass with cattle brought into the area.
